Overall Performance

The Doc Readiness program is delivering measurable improvement across both Internal and External experts (Without SD). Average doc readiness rose +6.2 pp (Internal) and +7.4 pp (External) versus the Feb 1–Mar 8 baseline, while the share of engagements with <50% doc readiness fell by 4.7 pp (Internal) and 5.3 pp (External). Combined cohort data show the share above 50% readiness rising from 85.54% to 90.35% post roll-out.
