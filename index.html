Doc Readiness Program — Post-Launch Performance

Daily Insights — March 10 – 24, 2026

Executive Summary

Since the Doc Readiness program launched, key metrics have shown meaningful improvement across both Internal and External partner engagements:

  • Average Doc Readiness rose significantly — Internal partners improved from 73.60% to 79.90% (+6.3 pp), while External partners jumped from 71.58% to 78.99% (+7.4 pp).
  • Fewer low-quality engagements — The share of engagements below the 50% readiness threshold dropped from 14.46% to 9.16%, a reduction of 5.3 percentage points.
  • Healthier engagements score higher — For engagements above the 50% threshold, average readiness increased from 82.60% to 86.17% (+3.6 pp), indicating the program lifts quality across the board.
  • Internal % under 50 improved the most — Internal partner engagements below 50% dropped from 14.20% to 8.94%, while External improved from 15.73% to 10.08%.
