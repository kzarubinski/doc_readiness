Key Insight: CST (Customer Spend Time) measures the time an expert spends on the call with the customer — lower is more efficient. INTUIT handles 84% of total volume with an avg CST of 3.38 hrs. However, INTUIT has the highest share of Premium SKUs (53.1%), which naturally require longer handle times. When comparing CST, the SKU mix must be considered — see the SKU tab for a controlled comparison. MAGNIT shows the longest overall CST (4.09 hrs) but handles 72.5% Premium engagements. FOUNDEVER and Education at Work show shorter CST driven primarily by Basic-heavy portfolios (simpler cases).

tNPS by SKU: Basic and Deluxe tNPS are generally high across all partners (79-96 range). The biggest divergence appears in Premium, where MAGNIT leads at 88.5 while FOUNDEVER drops to 21.7. INTUIT's Premium tNPS of 81.9 is solid and competitive with JDA (82.2).
Methodology: Revenue per Opportunity = HC% × ARPC (Average Revenue Per Completed). Cost per Opportunity = CST (hrs) × $36/hr. Net Revenue = Revenue − Cost. ARPC: Basic = $233, Deluxe = $321, Premium = $495. Cost rate = $36/hr across all partners.
